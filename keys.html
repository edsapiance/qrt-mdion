Realized Track Record - Key Points

As of time of publication, the realized track record corresponds to:

  • Two markets traded:
  • E-mini SP500 futures on the CME
  • Eurostoxx50 on the Eurex

These markets are traded sequentially with a 2-hour overlap, from Monday 16:30 to Tuesday 21:00 London time:

  • Eurostoxx50: 8:00 to 16:30 London time
  • E-mini SP500: 14:30 to 21:00 London time

The strategy is out of the market for the remaining time.

For a capital of K$ under management, the maximum notional exposure is 2 x K$. It is sometimes, but not always, spread out, making the strategy akin to delta-neutral. The position is held for a maximum of 30 hours.

For 1 Billion USD under management, the program trades at most 0.25% of the daily Emini SP500 average daily volume in 2024, at most 3.75% of the daily eurostoxx50 average daily volume in 2024

Current execution modalities:

Upon receiving the order, the execution price is calculated as follows:

  • Price = (H+L)/2 of the next minute barchart after the minute the order is received
  • Multiplied by 2 basis points (bps)
  • Multiplied by daily 60-day volatility of returns in percentage

With both underlying markets showing a 16% yearly volatility of returns, execution costs amount to 2% of K$ in AUM. This is highly attractive for internalizers who can match this against their own other managers.
